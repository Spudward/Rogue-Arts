About Us


Our upcoming release, Family Heirloom, is a scary Stealth Horror game with exciting Sci-Fi elements. You are tasked with manoeuvering throughout a facility plagued with the infected remnants of humanity. Your goal is to discover the fate of your family, and if possible, rescue them from a fate worse than death.

Explore through the wretched remains of what used to be a laboratory to find the crucial, but limited, ingredients to create the life-saving vaccine. Be careful as you travel, as the infected will hear you wandering and will stop at nothing to chase you down and transform you into one of them.
